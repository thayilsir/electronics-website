Electronic Components

Resistors

Resistors limit the flow of electric current in a circuit. Their resistance is measured in ohms (Ω) and is indicated by color bands. They are used to control voltage and current levels.

Capacitors

Capacitors store and release electrical energy. They are used for filtering, timing, and power stabilization. Their capacity is measured in farads (F).

Transistors

Transistors amplify or switch electronic signals. They are fundamental building blocks of modern electronic devices and come in various types like BJT, FET, and MOSFET.

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is a popular microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P. It has 14 digital I/O pins, 6 analog inputs, a USB connection, and is perfect for beginners with its simple programming environment.

  • Microcontroller: ATmega328P
  • Operating Voltage: 5V
  • Digital I/O Pins: 14
  • Flash Memory: 32 KB

Raspberry Pi 4

The Raspberry Pi is a series of small single-board computers that can run a full operating system. It's great for more complex projects that require networking or multimedia capabilities.

  • Processor: Broadcom BCM2711
  • RAM: 2GB, 4GB or 8GB LPDDR4
  • Connectivity: Dual-band WiFi, Bluetooth 5.0
  • Ports: 2 × USB 3.0, 2 × USB 2.0

ESP32

The ESP32 is a series of low-cost, low-power system-on-a-chip microcontrollers with integrated Wi-Fi and dual-mode Bluetooth. Ideal for IoT projects.

  • Processor: Xtensa dual-core 32-bit LX6
  • Wireless Connectivity: Wi-Fi + Bluetooth
  • Ultra-low power consumption
  • Rich peripheral interface

Interview Questions

Difference between analog and digital signals

Analog signals are continuous and represent physical measurements, while digital signals are discrete and represent data as binary values (0s and 1s).

Ohm's Law

Ohm's Law states that the current through a conductor is directly proportional to the voltage and inversely proportional to the resistance: V = I × R.

Purpose of a capacitor

Capacitors store electrical energy, filter noise from signals, block DC while allowing AC to pass, and are used in timing circuits.
